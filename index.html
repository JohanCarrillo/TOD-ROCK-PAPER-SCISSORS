The Rules
The rules of the fight are very simple so you, naive creature can understand them. The equivalent of Rock is a Cat Punch, the equivalent of Paper is a Cat Slap and the equivalent of Scissors is a Cat Scratch, so as the same way Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper and Paper beats Rock, the rules are:
  • Cat Punch beats Cat Scratch
  • Cat Scratch beats Cat Slap
  • Cat Slap beats Cat Punch
We'll be playing and the first one to get 3 victories wins. If we, cats, win, your pathetic race will be comdemned to give us all your croquettes. On the other hand, in the remote case that you win, I will allow you to rub my back.
